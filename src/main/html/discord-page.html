Emperial Recruiting Division

All are welcome to join The Emperium but to do so you must join our recruitment discord.

Here, you will submit your application, and if it is approved, you will be interviewed by a recruiter. After the interview we will then decide if you are fit for The Emperium.

If you are not responded too within 2 weeks of submitting your application it is denied, feel free to re-apply every other week. If you are accepted you will be invited to The Emperium Discord.

The Emperium

Here, after you've been accepted as a recruit and given the rank Sigma, you will be able to chat with the other members of The Emperium.

This discord is the main way we communicate as a group so it's imperative that you are active in text chat and voice chat.

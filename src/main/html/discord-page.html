Emperial Recruiting Division

All are welcome to join The Emperium but to do so you must join our recruitment discord.

Here, you will submit your application, and if it is approved, you will receive an interview. After the interview we will then decide if you are fit for Emperium.

If you are not responded too within 2 weeks of submitting your application it is denied, feel free to re-apply every other week. If you are accepted you will be invited to The Emperium Discord and given the rank Sigma.

About us

The emperium is the premiere group on 2b and one of the oldest groups on the server.

Most people know about us from our spawn presence or our involvement in the incursions, but we’re much more than just a PvP group.

We’ve organized incursions, single handedly lead the purge and created numerous clients and bots all while making some of the best and biggest bases on 2b.

The Emperium has three main focus’ on 2b building, pvp, and coding. Many of our more notable accomplishments on these three focus’ are on this website.
